Essay 2: Global economics and artistic implications of the web technologies and design techniques used :

The widespread adoption of web technologies has significantly impacted global economics and artistic expression. This essay examines the intersection of user-centric design, accessibility, and data visualization interactivity in my 'Hacking' website, exploring how these elements foster engaging learning experiences. Through a critical analysis of the site's UI/UX design and content curation, I will reflect on the challenges and successes of the project, assessing its alignment with international web development best practices. Ultimately, this research aims to demonstrate the potential of web technologies to promote inclusive, interactive, and informative digital experiences, ones that prioritizes design driven by a user-centred approach that caters to diverse needs and preferences.

The Impact of Web Technologies on Global Economics and Artistic Expression:

Web technologies have had a huge influence on global economies, specifically with regards to how they’ve provided a platform for users around the world to gain insight into the world around them. Specifically, this has been through allowing users to access information, connect with others, and express themselves creatively. With over 4.4 billion internet users worldwide (ITU, 2022), web technologies have enabled Global connectivity and community building through social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, Instagram etc.). Furthermore, they have also allowed access to educational resources and online courses (e.g., Coursera, edX) as well as provide opportunities for digital entrepreneurship and creative expression (e.g., Etsy, YouTube). Moreover, the growth of the gig economy, with 57 million freelancers in the US alone (Upwork, 2022) demonstrates the extensive impact of the opportunities that web technologies have created for users to express themselves creatively in the digital space, leveraging the tools available to them like the technologies to increase their global audiences. By empowering users to showcase their talents and creativity, web technologies have democratized access to artistic expression and facilitated the emergence of new forms of digital art, music, and literature.
