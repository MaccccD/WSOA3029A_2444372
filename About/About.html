About

For this project, I chose to explore the theme of 'Hacking' within the cyber security space. Driven by my fascination with the 'dark' side of the internet, I aim to raise awareness about the negative impacts of hacking on victims and vulnerable individuals. Through interactive and illustrative visualizations, I hope to tell a story about specific types of hacking work, empowering users to be more cautious and safe online. My wire-frames organize the content into two main primary sections apart from the design , style guide , theory and home section and these are: 1. Phishing: Exploring the types of phishing attacks and the extent to which it has affected people’s lives. 2. Ransomware: Delving into different forms of ransom notes and the number of victims that get experience the effects of ransomware. Each section will employ unique data visualization techniques to explain the hacking aspect, its impact, and provide valuable insights for users. By shedding light on these critical issues, I hope this site becomes educational and inspires users to adopt safer online practices.
